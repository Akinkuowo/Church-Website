FAQs

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use and safeguard when you use our church management app.

How is my personal information protected within the app?

We use industry-standard encryption and data protection measures to safeguard your members data and financial information is secured. Only authorized church staff and service providers have access to your data, it is never shared or sold to third parties.

Can i control activities on the web app?

Yes! You can customize your privacy settings to control who can see your profile, contact information, and participation in events or ministries. you can choose to share with staff, group members or keep your details private.

Can i track every activities on the app?

Yes! The app allows you to view and track every activity, people, events, media, giving, and others.

How can i access the reports & Analytics?

Report & analytics is available in each menu item on the app. You can view and filter reports and analytics of people, events, finances, media including analytics of the custom mobile app usage. The information is confidential and only accessable to you and authorized church members.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please reach out to our support team.
